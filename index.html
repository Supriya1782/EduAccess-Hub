SDG Goal 4: Quality Education

Empowering Every Student with Free Education

A web solution designed to bridge the educational gap for rural and underprivileged students by providing free structured resources and doubt-solving support.

📋 Project Documentation

1. Problem Statement

Desh ke rural aur economically weaker sections ke students ke paas quality study material aur proper guidance ki bhari kami hai, jisse dropout rates badhte hain.

2. Project Objective

Ek aisa open-source web application platform banana jo har bache ko bina kisi fees ke structured board-level notes aur peer mentorship connect de sake.

3. Solution Description

EduAccess Hub lightweight hai. Yeh responsive design ke sath free regional notes module aur ek real-time interactive doubt-solving forum offer karta hai.
